Licensed TCM Pharmacists & Advanced GMP Workshop

The Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) GMP workshop is equipped with standardized and clean labs, raw material warehouses, and finished product warehouses. In terms of production equipment, the workshop is furnished with modern equipment such as herb washing machines, vacuum herb moistening machines, herb cutting machines, electric heating herb frying machines, mesh belt dryers, hot air circulation ovens, and steaming and boiling cabinets.


For production management, a stringent quality control system is implemented. Both the production manager and the quality manager are licensed TCM pharmacists with extensive experience in managing the production of TCM decoction pieces, ensuring quality from the production stage.
