Chinese Angelica

⌈Aliases⌋ Qin gui, Yun gui, Xi danggui, Min danggui.
⌈Habits⌋ Prefers high-altitude, cold and low-temperature, long daylight areas.
⌈Characteristics of Chinese Angelica⌋ is the authentic production area of danggui. Yunnan Herbal Medicine chooses the planting base for danggui at an altitude of about 2600 meters on the sunny slope, most suitable for danggui growth. The medicinal properties of danggui are stable, with a strong medicinal flavor. According to testing, the content of ferulic acid in the danggui planted by the company is around 0.20%, which is four times the standard content of 0.05% specified in the Chinese Pharmacopoeia, comparable to Min danggui.

Costus Root

⌈Aliases⌋ Qingmuxiang, Guangmuxiang, Xingqucao.
⌈Habits⌋ Thrives in high-altitude cold mountainous areas above 2500 meters.
⌈Characteristics of Yunnan Herbal Medicine Costus Root⌋ Jianchuan is the authentic production area of Yunnan Muxiang. Yunnan Herbal Medicine's planting base is located in the Xiangtu Mountain area, which has advantages in altitude, climate, and other aspects. Therefore, the Yunnan Muxiang produced here is characterized by its solid texture, strong aroma, and high oil content, making it superior in quality and medicinal properties.

xuduan

⌈Alias⌋ Chuan Xuduan, Monk's Head, Chuan named "Bone Grass".
⌈Habit⌋ Prefers a cooler and more humid climate, dislikes high temperatures! Thrives in deep and fertile sandy soil rich in organic matter.
⌈Characteristics of Yunnan Herb Xuduan⌋ Jianchuan is the authentic production area of Xuduan. The planting base of Yunnan Herb Xuduan is located in high-altitude cold and rainy mountainous areas with an average annual temperature of 11℃. The soil here is loose, fertile, and rich in organic matter, providing an excellent environment for the growth of Xuduan. The Xuduan stems grown here are thick, soft in texture, and of superior quality.
