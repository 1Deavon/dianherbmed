Why US?

Yunnan Dianben Herb Industry Co., Ltd. has been committed to cultivating high-quality traditional Chinese medicinal herbs since 2017. We operate over 5,000 acres of green planting bases across high-altitude, low-latitude regions in northwest Yunnan, including Lijiang and Jianchuan. This unique environment, influenced by the Indian Ocean monsoon, offers ideal conditions for growing premium herbs, free from pests and pollution.
Our company has established a germplasm resource nursery, focusing on genetic diversity and biological characteristics to select top-quality seeds. We specialize in the cultivation of Angelica, Gentian, Platycodon, Aucklandia, Astragalus, and Dipsacus, among others. Collaborating with leading agricultural institutions, we have developed advanced techniques for planting, pest control, and harvesting, ensuring our herbs are of the highest quality.
At Yunnan Dianben Herb Industry, we blend traditional knowledge with modern science to produce superior medicinal herbs, supporting the health and well-being of our customers.

Farmland

Since 2017, Yunnan Dianben Herb Industry Co., Ltd. has been dedicated to cultivating high-quality traditional Chinese medicinal herbs. We have successfully leased nearly 10,000 acres of land in the northwest regions of Yunnan, including Wenhail Village in Baisha Town, Yulong County, Lijiang, Qinghua Village in Jianchuan, Laojun Mountain Town in Jianchuan, Machang in Heqing, and Niujie Township in Eryuan. Out of this, we have established over 5,000 acres of green planting bases for traditional Chinese medicine.
Our bases are located in the northwest Yunnan region, known for its high altitude and low latitude. This area is influenced by the Indian Ocean monsoon, resulting in a unique temperate monsoon climate characterized by dry winters, wet summers, distinct wet and dry seasons, small annual temperature differences, and large daily temperature fluctuations. The annual average temperature is 12.3°C. The unique geographic and climatic conditions, including high solar radiation, abundant rainfall, and cool temperatures, provide an excellent environment for the growth of high-quality medicinal herbs. The region's long sunshine hours and minimal pest and disease occurrences, combined with pollution-free soil, ensure that the herbs grown here are of exceptional quality, with high content and no pesticide or heavy metal residues. Lijiang Yulong County and its surrounding areas, known as the "Hometown of Yunnan Medicine," have a rich history of medicinal herb cultivation.
